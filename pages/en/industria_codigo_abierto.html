OPEN-SOURCE INDUSTRIES

Type: Academic project (Undergraduate thesis)
Team: Group work
Status: Unbuilt
Location: Plaza Huincul & Cutral Có, Neuquén, Argentina
Year: 2023
Project brief: Hydrocarbon industrial cities have historically been conceived as strictly extractive models, leading to significant social, environmental and territorial impacts. A transition toward a more sustainable and resilient model requires a paradigm shift that embraces new, integrated and gradual logics grounded in current realities.
Tools: AutoCAD – SketchUp – V-Ray – Photoshop – Illustrator – InDesign – Enscape
