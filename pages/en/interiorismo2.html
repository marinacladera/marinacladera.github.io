INTERIOR DESIGN

Type: Design, BIM development, and visualization of kitchen and bathroom spaces.
Work mode: Individual
Status: Built
Location: Florida, USA
Year: 2025
Objective: Manage the entire BIM process for a single-family home, from modeling existing conditions using point clouds to the final delivery of documentation. The work included developing and adjusting layouts in Revit, preparing graphic deliverables (plans, budgets, and renders), and remotely coordinating with international teams, combining interior design and technology to optimize workflow.
Tools: Revit, Enscape, AI, Point cloud (Meshlab – ReCap).
