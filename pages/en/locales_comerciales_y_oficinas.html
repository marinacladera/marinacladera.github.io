COMMERCIAL PREMISES AND OFFICES

Type: Commercial premises and offices
Work mode: Individual
Status: Unbuilt
Location: Córdoba, Argentina
Year: 2022
Objective: Design of a two-level building with commercial premises and offices on a lot between party walls measuring 20 meters in width by 35 meters in depth. The program includes two commercial spaces ranging from 40m² to 50m², each with its own bathroom, two office units, a staircase core, and parking spaces both at the front and rear of the property.
Tools: Revit/MEP/Str – 3Ds Max – V-Ray – Illustrator – Photoshop
