SOCIO-ENVIRONMENTAL CONDENSER

Type: Academic project (Architecture V)
Team: Group work
Status: Unbuilt
Location: La Cañada Creek area, Córdoba, Argentina
Year: 2020
Project brief: Natural systems and residual spaces as support for ecosystemic interventions, embracing new paradigms aimed at transforming and improving the quality of the collective habitat. Strategies and proposals at territorial, urban and architectural scales within a city system.
Tools: AutoCAD – SketchUp – V-Ray – Photoshop – Illustrator – InDesign
